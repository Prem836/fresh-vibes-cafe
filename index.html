Welcome to Fresh Vibes Café

Nestled along the National Highway at Chadwal More in Kathua, Fresh Vibes Café is a haven for food lovers who appreciate the purity and richness of vegetarian Indian cuisine. We are a complete family restaurant serving hearty breakfasts, wholesome lunches, and satisfying dinners.

From sizzling snacks and aromatic main courses to refreshing drinks and indulgent desserts — every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and love, staying true to our promise: 100% Pure Vegetarian.
